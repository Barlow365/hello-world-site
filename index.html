What is The Grid?

The Grid is an upcoming L1/L2 blockchain designed for builders who care about sustainability, user experience, and clear value flows—not just token charts.

Built for products

Optimized for applications that need real users: wallets, payments, media, membership, identity, and more.

Predictable economics

A fee model and incentive structure that makes sense to non-crypto teams, with transparent costs and clear upside for long-term builders.

Ecosystem support

Access to education, co-marketing, and technical guidance to get from idea → MVP → real-world rollout.
