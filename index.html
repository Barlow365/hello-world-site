Overview

The Grid is an L1/L2-optimised execution layer focused on predictability and real-world economics. We provide fast finality, developer‑friendly tooling, and a fee model designed for products rather than speculation.

We’re building for teams that need reliability, low latency, and clear operational costs — from creators and startups to enterprises exploring programmable ownership and identity.

Our approach reduces friction and helps teams ship: minimal developer surface area, straightforward integrations, and deterministic performance for production workloads.

Why build on The Grid

Throughput

High parallel execution and batched settlement for both micro and macro workloads.

Low latency

Near-instant confirmations for UX-critical paths — payments, identity checks, and realtime membership actions.

Security & predictability

Clear, auditable incentive structures and a predictable fee model for long-term builders.

Developer experience

Simple APIs, sane defaults, and developer tooling focused on shipping production products quickly.
